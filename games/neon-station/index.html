🚀 Neon Station

Neon arena shooter with enemy classes, projectiles, shotgun bursts, pickups, and a boss wave.

Keyboard, mouse, touch, and virtual controls are supported.

How to Play

  • Goal: Survive 10 waves, defeat drones/sentinels/phantoms, and destroy the boss sentinel
  • Win reward: Neon Station Wave-10 Emblem.
  • Use WASD/arrow keys, mouse/touch aim, Fire/Alt buttons, Pause and Restart.
